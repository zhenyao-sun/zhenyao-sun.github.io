Summer Symposium on Stochastic Systems 2026

Random Systems · Stochastic Analysis · Interactive Dynamics

Overview

SSSS 2026 is the second edition of the Summer Symposium on Stochastic Systems, a one-week workshop hosted by the Probability Group at Beijing Institute of Technology. The symposium features invited research talks and mini-courses on stochastic analysis, stochastic PDEs, and related topics.
